Renewed Spirit

A peaceful atmosphere paired with exquisite cuisine is the essence of The Forest Kitchen & Bar.

Jaipur offers numerous dining spots to delight your palate, but The Forest Kitchen & Bar is for those who believe that “great food is best enjoyed in a refined setting.” As you walk through the entrance, you’ll feel as if you’re passing through a dense tree-lined tunnel that opens into a stunning area filled with lush greenery. The gentle background music, soft lighting, and overall ambiance transport you away from the city’s hustle and bustle.

Let’s face it, while we can satisfy our hunger with food deliveries, stepping out for a meal is about relishing an environment that refreshes our mind, making dining both special and safe. The Forest Kitchen & Bar understands this well, offering an unparalleled fine dining experience amidst nature's bounty. Here's a curated selection of experiences along with a comprehensive menu of Drinks and Dine options.

Kitchen

After taking in the surroundings, the next delight is the food!

The Forest Kitchen & Bar offers an extensive selection of flavorful dishes, ranging from well-seasoned vegetarian dishes to a diverse array of non-vegetarian options. The tempting cuisine will keep you coming back for more, leaving you already planning your next visit.

Bar

As the day winds down, guests can relax at this bar in a chic, modern ambiance. The Bar remains open late into the night and offers craft beers and an international selection of popular cocktails. You can also enjoy in-house music to set the mood, with occasional live screenings of sports events and live performances by guest bands.

The bar is alive with innovative mixology concepts. Popular drinks like the Absent Without Leave or the Three Wise Men are a treat for any palate.

Microbrewery

The Forest’s Microbrewery typically offers six craft beers on tap, perfect for enjoying alongside your meal. You can sample different craft beers to find your favorite. Make your days and nights unforgettable with Rajasthan's premier microbrewery!
